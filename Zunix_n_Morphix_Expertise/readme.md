How to receive actual emails:
Right now, the form is functionally perfect but is doing a "simulated" submit because it doesn't know where to send the emails. To connect it to your inbox for free without needing a backend server:

1. Go to Web3forms
2. Enter your email to get a free Access Key.
3. Open contact.html and replace YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE (on line 118) with your new key.
