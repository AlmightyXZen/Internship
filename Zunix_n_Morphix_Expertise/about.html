Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

Zunix n Morphix Expertise was established as a web designing company in July 2008. We started working with US-based outsourced projects, and our first live project was a website for Rayat Shikshan Sanstha's College situated in Panvel.

In 2009, we expanded our expertise into Software Development on VB.Net platform, creating customized software applications for billing and inventory management. Today, we offer comprehensive web services and software solutions to businesses across various industries.
